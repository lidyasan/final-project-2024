Hispanic Data Project

This is a narrative documentation of educational and employment progression in the hispanic/latino demographic. When I was young, I remember being told by a teacher at a young age that only 15% of us would have the opportunity to go to college.  Many of my peers did not pursue a college education.
I believe the way we  talk to younger generations is vital in their formation of beliefs in regard to what is possible and what isn't.
